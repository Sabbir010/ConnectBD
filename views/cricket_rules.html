How to Play Cricket

Game Rules & System

1. Creating a Team

  • To play, you first need a team. Creating a team costs 200 Gold Coins.
  • A team consists of 1 captain and up to 4 other players.
  • The captain can send invitations to other users to join their team.

2. Challenging Another Team

  • Only a team captain can challenge another team to a match.
  • You can set a bet amount using either your account balance or Gold Coins.
  • The challenged team's captain must accept the challenge for the match to begin.

3. How to Play

  • The match starts with a toss. Captains choose Heads or Tails.
  • The toss-winning captain decides whether to bat or bowl first.
  • Each turn, both the current batsman and bowler must submit a sequence of digits (1, 2, 3, 4, 6) within 90 seconds.
  • If the batsman's digit matches the bowler's digit for any ball, the batsman is OUT.
  • If the digits don't match, the number submitted by the batsman is added to their score.
  • An over consists of 6 balls. The game is 5 overs per side.

4. Timeout Rules

  • If only the Batsman times out: The batsman is OUT, and the bowling team receives 5 penalty runs. One ball is deducted from the over.
  • If only the Bowler times out: The batsman gets their submitted runs PLUS 5 penalty runs. The over is immediately finished, and the next over will start.
  • If BOTH players time out: The batsman is OUT, and the batting team gets 15 penalty runs.

5. Winning & Prizes

  • The team with the higher score at the end wins the match.
  • The prize money (from the bet) is distributed among the winning team members.
  • Important: Any player who causes a timeout will NOT receive any prize money, even if their team wins.
